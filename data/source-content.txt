CONTENT WRITING STANDARDS
Write for humans first, then optimize for search.
Lead with the answer before expanding on details.
Keep paragraphs concise and easy to scan.
Use active voice and plain language.
Demonstrate experience with real examples, case studies, and measurable outcomes.
Focus on benefits and business impact rather than features alone.
Ensure every page has a clear next step for the visitor.
