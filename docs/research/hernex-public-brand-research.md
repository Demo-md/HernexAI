## Verified Positioning Summary

The strongest publishable direction is a women-led marketing and branding partner that combines human strategy and creative work with practical AI-enabled execution. The publicly described audience includes founders, coaches, small businesses, and women-driven ventures. The public service range supports a concise marketing, brand, content, website, advertising, email, and automation story.

## Confirmation Queue Before Copywriting

1. Confirm preferred legal/public name and casing: `HerNexAI`, `HerNex AI`, or `HerNex AI Marketing`.
2. Approve final founder bio, geography, contact details, business-hours information, and service availability.
3. Supply approved logo files, colour use, font licence/choice, photography, social accounts, and legal links.
4. Approve any client names, logos, testimonials, campaigns, work samples, permissions, dates, and measurable results.
5. Confirm whether Growth Technology Services, AI agents, apps, cloud, CRM/ERP, and security services are active, ownable offers or only repository drafts.
6. Confirm privacy, cookie, terms, booking, and analytics requirements before forms or tracking are built.

## Research Limitations

- LinkedIn is company-controlled public content, not independent verification of capability or outcomes.
- Search engines imposed bot protection or did not return reliable independent sources during this research pass.
- No approved official site, Instagram, Facebook, portfolio, business listing, brand kit, or case-study permission pack was identified from the accessible evidence.
- This document never treats follower count, team size, post engagement, job listings, or promotional numerical claims as evergreen website proof.
