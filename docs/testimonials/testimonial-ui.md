# Testimonial UI

## Homepage

The fourth capability section retains its strategy heading, supporting copy, and CTA. Its former overlapping concept sheets are replaced by three compact verified-review rows.

- All three reviews are visible without a carousel.
- Each row has initials, name, business or service label, accessible five-star label, preview text, and native `details` expansion for the complete review.
- Full review wording remains in the DOM and is keyboard accessible.
- Hover and focus move a row by at most two pixels and strengthen its divider. Reduced motion keeps all rows stable.

## Capabilities Page

The former `A note on evidence` statement was removed. The replacement section uses an editorial heading and three compact columns, intentionally distinct from the homepage proof list.

## Accessibility

- Review quotes use `blockquote`; reviewer attribution uses `cite`.
- Stars expose `5 out of 5 stars`; visual star characters are hidden from assistive technology.
- No review content depends on hover, auto-scrolling, or animation.
- Source-review controls are intentionally withheld until the actual screenshot sources are present.
