# About Page Content

## Final Structure

1. Founder-led opening: HerNexAI's purpose, Priyanka Jha's name and role, LinkedIn action, and a clearly marked portrait-approval placeholder.
2. Founder story: verified professional focus, an AI-with-human-judgement explanation, and one founder statement marked for approval.

4. Closing CTA: Start a conversation and Explore our services.

## Approval Boundary

The statement “Technology should make good thinking move faster-not make the work feel less human.” is proposed website copy. It is labelled `Founder statement - approval required` in the interface and must not be treated as a verified historical quotation until Priyanka Jha approves it.

## Excluded Content

No unverified experience length, client total, awards, revenue, team size, employment history, personal biography, campaign outcome, or qualification claim is used.
