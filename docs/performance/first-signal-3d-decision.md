# First Signal 3D Decision

## Why Three.js Is Justified

The requested story is a signal field becoming organised and converging into the gold signal that starts the HN Growth System. A tiny depth-aware constellation communicates that transition more clearly than a static decorative gradient while remaining separate from the existing DOM/SVG hero system.

## Limits

- Homepage first visit only; no route-transition replay.
- Lazy-loaded after the overlay eligibility check.
- 24 desktop / 14 mobile points, shared buffer geometries and materials, two draw calls.
- No models, textures, lights, shadows, post-processing, mouse effects, or persistent renderer.
- DPR cap: `1.5` desktop and `1` mobile.
- The scene pauses in hidden tabs and is disposed at hero handoff.

## Fallback

The navy overlay, gold dot, and the real server-rendered hero remain usable without WebGL. Any renderer failure immediately continues the GSAP handoff; a hard timeout prevents a stalled introduction.
