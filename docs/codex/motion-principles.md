# HernexAI Motion Principles

- Plan motion as a sequence with a purpose: orient, reveal, explain, confirm, or transition.
- Use a small hierarchy: one signature moment, a few supporting reveals, and quiet microinteractions.
- Prefer transform and opacity; avoid layout-driven animation.
- Keep motion reversible, interruptible, and safe during route changes and reverse scroll.
- Never hide essential content until animation completes.
- Use `prefers-reduced-motion` to provide a stable static or low-motion state.
- Pause autonomous loops when offscreen or when the tab is hidden.
- Use Lenis only when the experience benefits from it and preserve native scroll semantics.
