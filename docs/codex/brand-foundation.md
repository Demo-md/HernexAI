# HernexAI Brand Foundation

- Verified direction from project briefs: premium, modern, professional, human-led marketing and branding with AI as an enabler.
- Preferred identity direction: HernexAI navy and gold, used with restraint and adequate contrast.
- Use supplied repository assets and verified content when available. Do not manufacture logo variants, client proof, awards, results, testimonials, or statistics.
- Keep the brand warm and capable; avoid generic neon cyberpunk, rainbow gradients, and AI symbolism as the entire identity.
- If an asset, color value, typeface, or claim is not verified in the repository, label it as missing or provisional rather than silently inventing it.
