# Service Taxonomy

`src/data/site.ts` is the single public service catalogue.

- Marketing: eight preserved services and existing detail URLs.
- Technology: 22 structured capabilities exposed from `/services/technology`.
- Legacy route: `/services/tech-services` remains a preserved Growth Technology Services detail page.
- Each catalogue item includes category, slug, summary, problem, solution, deliverables, tags, chatbot context, WhatsApp context, evidence key, and related capabilities.

The header and mobile menu only expose the two category hubs. They do not duplicate individual service lists.
