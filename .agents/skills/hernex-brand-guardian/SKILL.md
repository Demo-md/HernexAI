# Hernex Brand Guardian

Use whenever a public-facing design, copy, asset, proof point, or CTA is created or reviewed. Read `docs/codex/brand-foundation.md`, `references/hernex-brand-rules.md`, and `references/content-integrity-checklist.md`.

## Directives

- Preserve the verified navy-and-gold direction unless repository evidence or a user-approved brief changes it.
- Keep the tone premium, modern, professional, human-led, and useful. Treat AI as an enabler, not the whole identity.
- Use actual repository assets and supplied brand information. Keep logo treatment, typography, color usage, and voice consistent.
- Never invent clients, testimonials, awards, analytics, campaign results, business statistics, or performance figures.
- Never copy competitor/reference language, visuals, assets, code, or exact composition.
- Mark missing brand information, provisional copy, and placeholders explicitly. Do not make placeholders look like verified proof.

## Handoff

Report verified inputs, missing inputs, factual risks, asset provenance, and any required user confirmation. A visually polished unsupported claim is a brand-integrity failure.
