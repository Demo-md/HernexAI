# Content Integrity Checklist

- [ ] Every client/logo/testimonial is verified in repository content
- [ ] Every statistic has a source or is removed
- [ ] No awards, certifications, analytics, or campaign outcomes were invented
- [ ] Placeholder content is labeled and visually distinguishable from proof
- [ ] Claims are specific and supportable
- [ ] Reference-site language is paraphrased into original HernexAI language
- [ ] Image/asset provenance is known
- [ ] Missing brand information is reported instead of guessed
