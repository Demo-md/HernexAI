# Anti-Pattern Catalogue

- **Template hero:** centered heading, paragraph, two buttons, and a generic gradient without a specific story.
- **Card soup:** every section reduced to identical rounded cards with the same title/body/button structure.
- **Sloppy collage:** random rotation and offsets that imply energy but destroy hierarchy, alignment, or readability.
- **AI wallpaper:** glowing orbs, neural lines, purple-blue gradients, and dashboard fragments with no product meaning.
- **Proof theater:** invented logos, metrics, testimonials, awards, or client badges used to fill empty space.
- **Center lock:** every heading and paragraph centered even when a split or editorial layout would clarify the story.
- **Radius monotony:** the same large corner radius applied to every surface regardless of content role.
- **Motion confetti:** unrelated infinite loops and hover effects competing for attention.

Reject these patterns unless the user explicitly requires one and the design rationale is documented.
